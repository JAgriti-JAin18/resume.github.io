About Me

I am a dedicated researcher in water resources, focusing on urban flood resilience, socio-environmental systems, and the impacts of climate change. With a strong foundation in hydrology, geospatial analysis, and computational modeling, I aim to contribute to sustainable solutions for water resource management and climate adaptation.

Research

My research explores the dynamic interactions between hydrology, climate change, and urban systems. Key projects include:

  • Assessing Urban Community Resilience to Flood: Hybrid multi-criteria decision-making framework applied to Guwahati City, Assam, India.
  • Policy Improvement for Flood Disaster Management: Leveraging flood risk attributes to enhance governance frameworks in India.
  • Hydroclimatic Impacts on Socio-Environmental Systems: Investigating regime transitions in the Brahmaputra catchment under extreme events.
